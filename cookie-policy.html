What Are Cookies:

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, login information, and other settings.

How We Use Cookies:

We use cookies to analyze how users interact with our website and to improve its performance. We also use cookies to personalize your experience, such as remembering your language preference or login information.

We may use both session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which remain on your computer until they expire or are deleted.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions made by you, such as logging in or filling out forms.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings, such as your language preference or font size.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies help us to analyze how users interact with our website and to improve its performance. They collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often and if users receive error messages from certain pages.
  • Third-Party Cookies: We may also use third-party cookies, such as those from Google Analytics, to analyze how users interact with our website and to improve its performance. These cookies are subject to the privacy policies of their respective providers.

How to Control Cookies:

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. For more information, visit aboutcookies.org. You can also set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our website.
